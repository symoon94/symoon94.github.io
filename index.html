The Last Mile
Person

I'm Luna (Sooyoung Moon / 문수영), a Team Lead at Upstage AI where I lead the SolarBox team — packaging AI models for enterprise on-premise deployment.

I call myself "the last mile" person. I bridge engineering and business, making AI products not just functional, but actually usable. That means coordinating across teams, writing the glue code nobody wants to write, debugging the bugs nobody can reproduce, and shipping the things that need to ship.

I'm obsessed with AI agent automation — I've built team agents that deliver daily briefings, automate ops, and multiply what a small team can accomplish. I believe the future belongs to those who can orchestrate AI, not just build it.

Outside of work, I'm bilingual (Korean/English), endlessly curious, and believe in focusing on each moment with intention.
